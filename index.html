Jorge Aurelio Menéndez

About Me

I'm a graduate student in the CoMPLEX program at UCL, where I am currently working on my PhD at the Gatsby Computational Neuroscience Unit under the supervision of Peter Latham and Adam Kampff. I'm working on circuit-based models of motor learning. More broadly, I'm interested in the algorithms underlying biological learning.
